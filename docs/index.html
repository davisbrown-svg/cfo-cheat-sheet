⚡ Pro Tips from the Field

01
Start with Item 1A, not Item 7. Risk Factors are where management tells you what scares them. That's your compelling event menu. Read every single one.
02
Compare 3 years, not just the latest. A single year is a snapshot. Three years reveals trends — and trends are where the pressure lives.
03
Cross-reference MD&A with the numbers. If Item 7 says "operational efficiency" but SG&A went up 400bps, that's your opening — they know the problem, they don't have the answer.
04
Restructuring charges = urgency mode. If you see restructuring on the income statement, leadership is already cutting. Automation isn't a nice-to-have — it's the plan.
05
Search for "digital transformation" in the filing. If they're talking about it, they're spending on it. Get on the list before the budget is committed.
